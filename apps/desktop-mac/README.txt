FTC Sim Player (Mac)
====================

This is the Mac join client for FTC Sim multiplayer.

You cannot host matches from this app. Ask your friend (on Windows) to host,
then enter their address here — for example 192.168.1.5:5191 on the same Wi‑Fi,
or their public-ip:5191 if they forwarded TCP port 5191.

Download updates: https://github.com/Pleasantnoob/VibeCodedFTCSIM/releases
